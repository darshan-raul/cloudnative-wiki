# Domain 2: Design for New Solutions

This domain constitutes **29%** of the exam and focuses on designing architectures from scratch to meet specific business requirements.

## Key Areas
*   **2.1 Deployment Strategy**: IaC, CI/CD, and deployment patterns.
*   **2.2 Business Continuity**: Multi-AZ/Regional architectures and replication.
*   **2.3 Security Controls**: Application security and access management.
*   **2.4 Reliability Requirements**: Decoupling and scaling.
*   **2.5 Performance Objectives**: Compute selection and caching.
*   **2.6 Cost Optimization Strategy**: Data transfer and storage tiering.
