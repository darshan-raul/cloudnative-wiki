# Domain 1: Design Solutions for Organizational Complexity

This domain constitutes **26%** of the exam and focuses on multi-account strategies and complex networking.

## Key Areas
*   **1.1 Architect Network Connectivity Strategies**: Global infrastructure, hybrid connectivity, and traffic management.
*   **1.2 Prescribe Security Controls**: IAM, network security, and governance.
*   **1.3 Design Reliable and Resilient Architectures**: Disaster recovery and high availability.
*   **1.4 Design a Multi-Account AWS Environment**: AWS Organizations and centralized logging.
*   **1.5 Cost Optimization & Visibility**: Cost allocation and rightsizing.
